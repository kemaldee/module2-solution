Our Menu

Chicken

Chicken, a common poultry meat, is tender and flavorful, suitable for frying, grilling, boiling, or making soups. It's a good source of high-quality protein, selenium, and vitamin B6.

Beef

Beef is a popular meat globally, rich in protein, iron, zinc, and B vitamins. It's versatile, often cooked by grilling, boiling, or frying, offering various flavorful and nutritious dishes.

Sushi

Sushi, a traditional Japanese dish, consists of rice combined with fresh fish, seafood, eggs, or vegetables. It's widely enjoyed worldwide, known for its taste and health benefits, including omega-3 from fish.
